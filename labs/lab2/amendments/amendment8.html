Amendment VIII

"Excessive bail shall not be required, nor excessive fines imposed, nor cruel and unusual punishments inflicted."

Analysis

This amendment was created to prevent any punishments for crimes to not be excessive or cruel or for any barbaric penalities to be instilled. For example, setting a unusally large bail amount for a minor crime would be illegal under this amendment. This was to give citizens a proportionate discplining based on their crime and their ability to pay for them.
